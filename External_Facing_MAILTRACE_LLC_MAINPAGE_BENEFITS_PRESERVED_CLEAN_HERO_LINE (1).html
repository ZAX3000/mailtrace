Our Story & Mission

MailTrace was born out of a simple frustration: businesses were pouring money into direct mail campaigns but had no real way to measure if the effort was working. Traditional marketing dashboards left direct mail out of the equation, and tracking ROI often required guesswork.

Our mission is clear: to bring transparency, accuracy, and modern analytics to direct mail marketing. By combining proven marketing principles with innovative technology, MailTrace helps businesses finally see the full picture—every mail piece, every conversion, every dollar working harder.

About us

MailTrace was founded by industry professionals who wanted to bring the analytic capabilities of digital ads to direct mail for their clients. Despite direct mails 7% response rate compared to 0.5% in digital, the ability to show what jobs matched which mail campaigns wasn’t possible. Through the use of proprietary AI software, we are able to deliver rich data analytics we could only have dreamed of years ago. We are committed to helping you grow your business and be a partner wherever you have questions or suggestions!

FAQ

How is MailTrace priced?

MailTrace is $50 per month plus $0.045 per mail piece analyzed. That covers verification, tracking, and analytics for each mail record — giving you complete visibility into what’s working and what’s wasted.

Why do you charge per mail piece?

Every mail piece is verified and analyzed individually. Think of the 4.5¢ as insurance for your marketing dollars — it ensures every address is valid, every record is tracked, and every insight is accurate, so you only pay for results that actually drive revenue.

What does the $50 monthly subscription include?
  • KPI dashboard (Mail Sent, Match %, Conversion Rate)
  • Campaign analytics and match-back reports
  • Address verification tools
  • Data exports and performance tracking
  • Dedicated support and updates
Are there setup, onboarding, or cancellation fees?

No. MailTrace is month-to-month — no contracts, no hidden fees, and no risk.

What kind of data and insights will I get?
  • Which customers responded to your mailers
  • Conversion rates and total jobs generated
  • ROI for each campaign
  • Verified vs. invalid addresses
  • Year-over-year performance trends

It’s not about how many postcards you send — it’s about which ones drive business.

How does MailTrace collect these insights?

When you upload your mailing list and customer/job data as CSV files, MailTrace runs a secure match-back analysis. Each record is cross-referenced and verified against your completed jobs to show exactly which campaigns generated revenue — giving you clear, measurable ROI from every mail piece.

How accurate is MailTrace’s data?

MailTrace uses proprietary verification and match algorithms that typically reach 95–99% accuracy, depending on the quality of your data.

Who is MailTrace designed for?

MailTrace was built for the home service industry — plumbers, roofers, HVAC companies, landscapers, painters, and any local business that relies on direct mail to generate new jobs.

Does MailTrace replace my direct mail vendor?

No — we work alongside your existing vendor. They handle printing and mailing; MailTrace handles the analytics and ROI tracking.

Do you work with agencies or enterprises?

Our current focus is on the home service industry, but support for marketing agencies and enterprise mail vendors is with flexible, custom pricing.

How do I get started?
  1. Sign up for an account.
  2. Upload your mailing and customer lists (CSV files).
  3. MailTrace analyzes, verifies, and delivers your campaign insights automatically.
Can you help me interpret my data?

Absolutely. Our support team will walk you through your dashboard, explain your KPIs, and help you identify trends or campaign opportunities.

How secure is my data?

Security is built into MailTrace. All files are encrypted in transit and at rest, hosted on secure U.S.-based servers. We never sell or share your data — ever.
